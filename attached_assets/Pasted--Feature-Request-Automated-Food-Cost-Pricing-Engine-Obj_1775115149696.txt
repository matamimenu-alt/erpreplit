### 🔧 Feature Request: Automated Food Cost & Pricing Engine

**Objective:**
Build an automated food cost calculation and smart pricing system that integrates with existing purchase and expense data.

---

### 📥 Inputs Required:

1. Dish ingredients (entered manually per item)
2. Quantity of each ingredient used per dish
3. Existing purchase data (cost per unit already stored in system)
4. Fixed monthly expenses (already stored)
5. Staff salaries (already stored)

---

### ⚙️ System Logic:

#### 1. Ingredient Cost Calculation:

* Fetch ingredient cost from purchase database
* Calculate total ingredient cost per dish based on quantity used

#### 2. Add Waste Percentage:

* Apply food waste percentage (default: 5–10%)

#### 3. Allocate Fixed Costs:

* Distribute fixed expenses + salaries across dishes based on:

  * Total monthly sales OR
  * Number of orders (configurable)

#### 4. Delivery Cost Allocation:

* Average delivery cost per order: **7 SAR**
* Minimum order value: **30 SAR**
* System should distribute delivery cost proportionally per dish

#### 5. Target Food Cost Percentage:

* Default target: **25%**

---

### 🧮 Pricing Formula:

Final Dish Cost =
(Ingredient Cost + Waste + Allocated Fixed Costs + Allocated Delivery Cost)

Suggested Selling Price =
Final Dish Cost ÷ Target Food Cost % (25%)

---

### 📊 Output Required:

For each dish, the system should display:

* Total ingredient cost
* Waste cost
* Fixed cost allocation
* Delivery cost allocation
* Final cost per dish
* Suggested selling price
* Profit margin %
* Food cost %

---

### 📈 Advanced Feature:

* Show comparison with:

  * Average delivery app pricing (based on 25% commission assumption)
* Suggest:

  * Dine-in price
  * Delivery app price (higher to cover commission)

---

### ⚡ Optional Enhancements:

* Allow manual override of target food cost %
* Dynamic pricing suggestions (e.g. psychological pricing: 24.9 instead of 25)
* Profit simulator (adjust % and see price changes live)

---

### 🎯 Goal:

Enable automatic, accurate, and scalable menu pricing using real purchase data and operational costs.
