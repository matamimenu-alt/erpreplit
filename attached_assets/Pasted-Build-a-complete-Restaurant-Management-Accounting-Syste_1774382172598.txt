Build a complete **Restaurant Management & Accounting System** for Saudi Arabia using a simple web app (Python Flask + SQLite or Node.js + SQLite).

The system must include the following modules:

1️⃣ SALES MANAGEMENT
Create a page to record **daily sales excluding VAT (15%)**.

Fields:

* Date
* Food Sales
* Beverage Sales

Automatically calculate:

* Total daily sales
* Monthly totals
* Monthly sales summary

2️⃣ PURCHASE MANAGEMENT
Create a purchases section with:

Fields:

* Date
* Supplier Name
* Product Name
* Quantity
* Price
* Checkbox: Price includes VAT (15%)

The system must automatically calculate:

* Purchase amount before VAT
* VAT amount
* Total purchase amount

3️⃣ VAT CALCULATION (Saudi ZATCA)

Automatically calculate:

Output VAT = Sales × 15%
Input VAT = Purchases VAT

VAT Payable = Output VAT – Input VAT

Create a **VAT report page** showing:

* Total sales VAT
* Total purchase VAT
* VAT payable to the Saudi government.

4️⃣ SUPPLIER PRICE COMPARISON

Create a system that stores supplier product prices.

Show:

* Supplier
* Product
* Previous price
* Current price
* Price difference

Highlight price increases or decreases.

5️⃣ EMPLOYEE MANAGEMENT

Create an HR system including:

Employee Data:

* Employee Name
* Job Title
* Salary
* Iqama Expiry Date
* Iqama Renewal Date
* Last Travel Date
* Vacation Balance

Monthly cost calculation including:

* Accommodation cost
* Medical insurance
* Saudi GOSI insurance
* Air ticket cost divided by 12 months

Automatically calculate **total monthly employee cost**.

6️⃣ FIXED EXPENSES

Create a page for fixed monthly restaurant expenses.

Examples:

* Rent
* Electricity
* Internet
* Maintenance
* Service contracts

Fields:

* Expense name
* Monthly cost
* Contract start date
* Contract end date

* Total Food Sales
